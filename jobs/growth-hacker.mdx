## What you'll do:

- **Create content that people actually want to consume**—from engaging YouTube tutorials to deep-dive technical blog posts that turn readers into fans.
- **Build brand visibility** by scaling partnerships, sponsorships, and collaborations with top content creators.
- **Turn community engagement into rocket fuel** by organizing and running hackathons, meetups, and other community-driven events that bring people together.
- **Automate, hack, and build internal tools** to uncover powerful analytical insights and drive data-backed decision-making at scale.
- **Run rapid, scrappy, and experimental growth tests** across multiple channels—iterate fast, double down on what works, and kill what doesn’t.
- **Be Manchester-based (or ready to move! )**—this role thrives in an in-person, high-energy team environment.

## What you are responsible for:

- **Working side-by-side with the founder** to define and execute our growth strategy—you’re not just taking orders; you’re helping to shape the vision.
- **Scaling new growth channels (both organic & paid)**—SEO, ads , social, partnerships... the list is endless.
- **Owning and optimizing digital marketing efforts**—from email campaigns to ad funnels, you’ll turn ideas into high-impact campaigns.

## To crush it in this role, you'll need:

- **Experience with python and Excel** with a willingness to learn and improve your skills 
- **A numbers-driven mindset** - you measure everything, iterate fast and have a love for optimizing metrics 
- **Agility and adaptability** - when the landscape shifts, you move fast, pivot smartly and keep pushing forward. 

## You'll be a great fit if you:

- **Thrive in an in-person, high-energy environment**—remote is cool, but you love bouncing ideas in real life.
- **Are a creative builder**—whether it’s tools, content, or strategies, you turn ideas into reality.
- **Own your work like a founder**—no one needs to chase you down; you take full responsibility and run with it.
- **Love working in a fast-paced team**—startups move fast, and you’re here for the ride.
- **Are obsessed with learning**—you dive deep into new topics, master them quickly, and apply what you learn.
- **Have built something cool before**—ran a side hustle, grown a social media page, organized events? Show us what you’ve done!
