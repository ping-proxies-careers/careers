## What you'll do:

  - **Create content that stands out** - from deep-dive blog posts to snappy, shareable social media content that gets noticed. 
  - **Turn technical topics into must-read material** - write about VPNs, web scraping, and proxies in a way that educates and excites.
  - **Optimize landing pages and website content** - track performance, analyze engagement and tweak content strategies accordingly.
  - **Collaborate with designers & marketers** - bring content to life with visuals, infographics and strategic campaigns. 

## What you are responsible for:

  - **Owning the content strategy** —from ideation to execution, you’ll help build a library of high-impact content.
  - **Keeping our website and blog fresh**—regularly updating, optimizing, and maintaining existing content.
  - **Ensuring content is high-quality**—plagiarism-free, AI-detection-proof, and always aligned with our quality standards.
  - **Staying ahead of industry trends** - dig into the latest developments in VPN, web scraping and proxy services.

## To crush it in this role, you'll need:

  - **A Bachelor's degree** - English, Journalism, Communications, Marketing, or a related field.
  - **3+ years of writing experience** - you've been crafting high-quality content long enough to know what works.
  - **A solid grasp of VPNs, web scraping, and proxies** - you don't need to be an engineer, but you should understand the space.
  - **The ability to manage multiple projects** - deadlines don't scare you, and juggling tasks is second nature.
  - **Hands-on experience with content management systems (CMS) and web analytics tools** - you know how to organize, optimize, and track content performance like a pro.
  - **A good understanding of SEO fundamentals** - you can craft content that not only reads well but also ranks well.

## You'll be a great fit if you:
  - **Have a passion for tech** - you're genuinely interested in online privacy, proxies and data scraping.
  - **Thrive in a remote environment** - you're independent, proactive, and don't need constant hand-holding.
  - **Take ownership of your work** - you're detail-oriented, self motivated, and always looking to level up.
  - **Strong communication and collaboration skills** - you can work seamlessly with teams, give and receive feedback, and articulate ideas clearly.
